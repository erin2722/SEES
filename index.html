Welcome to Heat Waves and Vulnerable Communities! This application overlays extreme heat watches, warnings, and advisorys with the highly unemployed counties in the United States in order to identify vulnerable areas. This map considers a county with an unemployment rate over 16% to be vulnerable. After mapping the data, you can search for the current affected vulnerable counties by state. Use the toggle switches to turn the layers on and off, and click the bar on the left to open the search bar. The shelters demo displays all shelters in Baltimore, and in future versions there will be shelter data for every county.
